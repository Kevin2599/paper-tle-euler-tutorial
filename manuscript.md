Mathematical formulation
------------------------

Euler's equation,

$$
(x - x_0)\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} +
 (y - y_0)\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} +
 (z - z_0)\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z} =
 \eta (b - f).
$$
