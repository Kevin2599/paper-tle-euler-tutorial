Mathematical formulation
------------------------

Euler's equation,

\begin{equation}
(x_i - x_0)\dfrac{\partial f_i}{\partial x} +
 (y_i - y_0)\dfrac{\partial f_i}{\partial y} +
 (z_i - z_0)\dfrac{\partial f_i}{\partial z} =
 \eta (b - f_i).
\label{euler}
\end{equation}
